Never use em dashes (—) or en dashes (–) anywhere in the site UI. Always use regular hyphens (-).

**Why:** James's preference for clean, consistent punctuation across the app.

**How to apply:** In all JSX, string literals, placeholders, and error messages — replace — and – with -.
